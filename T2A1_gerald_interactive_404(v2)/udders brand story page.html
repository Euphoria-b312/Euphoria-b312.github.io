A LITTLE ABOUT US

In 2007, Udders Ice Cream was started in a tiny shop by our founders, David and Peck Lin.
Their mission was to fill the gap in the local Ice-Cream scene by creating flavours that would
satiate the taste buds of both locals and local alcoholics alike! Since then, Udders has developed
more than 30 Udderly unique flavours, blending local, international and even alcoholic ingredients! Yum!
